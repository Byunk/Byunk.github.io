## Career

[LinkedIn Profile](https://www.linkedin.com/in/kyungho-byoun/)

I currently work at [SAP](https://www.sap.com/about/what-is-sap.html) Labs Korea as a cloud engineer. My ongoing project involves developing a testing tool to facilitate database engineers in testing their new features within a cloud environment. Additionally, I am working with Kubernetes. I feel love with Kubernetes, even though I've only been studying it for two months.

From July, 2022 to June, 2023, I worked as a researcher at the [KI-ITC Augmented Reality Research Centre, KAIST](https://www.linkedin.com/company/kaistarrc/?originalSubdomain=kr). I analyzed cardiorespiratory signals from a bed pressure sensor to classify sleep stages. Also I developed a classification logic that will be embedded in a smart mattress. I utilized Matlab for signal analysis and Javascript for the classification logic.

In the second semester of 2022, I led a startup team called Treevial, consisting of 5 members. Our goal was to generate profits from forests through carbon credits to encourage afforestation by individuals and companies. We were finalists in App Startup Competition held by KAIST.

In between semesters, I worked twice as an undergraduate research intern at the [STRATEGIC AEROSPACE INITIATIVE Lab](http://sai.kaist.ac.kr/), KAIST. The main focus of my research was on routing problems, and I successfully reproduced few papers using Matlab.
